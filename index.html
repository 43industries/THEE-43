ABOUT US

CODE, COMMERCE, CLOTHING

43 Industries is deliberately two things at once: a technology studio that ships product for clients (apps, storefronts, integrations, and payment-sensitive flows), and a streetwear label that uses the same stack we recommend to others. The fashion side isn’t decoration—it’s where we rehearse inventory, checkout pressure, and launch storytelling before we bet a client’s revenue on it.

If you only know us through the tees, you’re seeing the brand lab. If you only know us through the repo, you’re seeing the engineering bench. Both run the same playbook: ship, measure, tighten, repeat—no vanity metrics we can’t back up with live work you can click through or try on.

Builds & integrations

Full-stack product, e-commerce, and fintech-adjacent flows— dashboards, APIs, and reliable checkouts for partners who outgrew guesswork.

Drops & identity

Limited runs, packaging, and visual systems—the cultural layer that keeps our technical standards honest about narrative and timing.

Proof, not padding

We’d rather send you to case studies, repos (where allowed), storefront URLs, and references than flash big years-active or project counts with no trail. Ask—we’ll point to what shipped.
